# Lab 10: RPZ Policy Builder

**Phase:** Modern DNS & Defensive Architecture | **Week:** 10

> Automated threat intelligence to Response Policy Zone pipeline.

---

## Overview

Designs and implements Response Policy Zones that integrate threat intelligence feeds (abuse.ch, PhishTank, Hagezi) into a BIND or Unbound resolver. Automates feed ingestion and RPZ zone generation.

## Tools

BIND9 or Unbound (VM), Python, threat intel feeds

## Deliverable

Automated RPZ pipeline + deployment documentation

## Reading

The Hidden Potential of DNS in Security — Ch. 11-12 + NIST SP 800-81
